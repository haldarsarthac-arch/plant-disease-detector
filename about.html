About LeafLens

Our mission, technology, and the people behind the project.

Our Mission

LeafLens was built with a simple goal: make plant health accessible to everyone — from backyard gardeners to professional agronomists. Plant diseases cause up to 40% of global crop losses annually, and early detection is critical.

We combine computer vision and a curated disease database to help you act fast, save your plants, and protect your harvest.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

LeafLens is designed as an educational and assistive tool. Diagnoses are AI-generated estimates based on visual patterns and should not replace professional agronomic advice. For critical crop management decisions, always consult a certified plant pathologist or agricultural extension service.
